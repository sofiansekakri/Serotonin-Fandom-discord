# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes the policies and procedures of **Serotonin** ("We", "Us", or "Our") regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you add or interact with our Discord bot application (the "Service").

By adding the Service to your Discord server or interacting with it, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

Because the Service operates entirely within the Discord platform, we do not collect browser data, hardware tracking IDs, or mobile device configurations. We only process data provided via the Discord API or directly by you.

### Data Collected Automatically

To function, the Service processes the following technical identifiers ("Snowflakes") provided by Discord:

* **Discord User IDs:** To identify unique users, process commands, and manage bot features.
* **Server (Guild) & Channel IDs:** To identify where commands are executed and where responses should be delivered.

### Data You Provide or Authorize

* **Command Content & Context:** Processing of the text commands you type to execute bot functions.
* **Interaction Data:** Configurations or settings you apply specifically to the bot within a server (e.g., custom prefixes, feature toggles).

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We process your data strictly for the following purposes:

* **Core Functionality:** To respond to commands, maintain server-specific settings, and ensure the bot operates as intended.
* **Service Maintenance:** To monitor performance, debug errors, and prevent abuse or exploitation of the bot.
* **Security:** To detect potential violations of Discord’s Terms of Service or our operational limits.

We **do not** sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties.

---

## 3. Data Retention and Deletion Schedule

We adhere to data minimization principles. We only store data for as long as it is operationally necessary.

## 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Your data is shared only under the following conditions:

* **Discord Platform:** Data inherently passes through Discord's infrastructure to transmit commands and responses.
* **Legal Compliance:** We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or a valid legal request by public authorities in Hessen, Germany.

---

## 5. Your Rights and Data Control

You have total control over the data the Service possesses:

* **Opt-Out/Removal:** You can remove the bot from your server at any time, which halts all data collection for that guild.
* **Data Erasure Requests:** You have the right to request the complete deletion of any stored configuration data associated with your User ID or Server ID.
* **How to Request Deletion:** You may request data removal by contacting us directly at **sofian1903@outlook.de**.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The Service complies with Discord's Terms of Service. We do not knowingly collect or maintain data from individuals under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain European jurisdictions). If we discover that a user under the required age has interacted with the bot, we will take immediate steps to purge their data from our temporary logs.
